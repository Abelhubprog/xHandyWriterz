## Executive Summary

HandyWriterz is a **domain-first editorial content platform** powered by Strapi 5. The platform serves two distinct surfaces:

1. **Public CMS Experience** â€” Editorial-quality content organized by specialized domains (Adult Nursing, AI, Crypto, etc.)
2. **User Dashboard** â€” Operational hub for orders, documents, messaging, and payments

Premium content is accessible via **x402 protocol** for agent-paid access.

## Design Principles

### 1. Editorial Quality
- Strong typography hierarchy
- Generous whitespace
- High-quality imagery from CMS
- Clean, readable layouts

### 2. CMS-First
- All public content from Strapi
- No hardcoded placeholder content
- Graceful empty states
- Real data or nothing

### 3. Domain-Centric
- Every piece of content belongs to a domain
- Domain pages are primary navigation targets
- Consistent domain theming (colors, icons)

### 4. Performance
- React Query caching
- Lazy-loaded routes
- Optimized images
- Minimal bundle size
